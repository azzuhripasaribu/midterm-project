these are some ways to avoid or prevent sexual violence against you.

try not to walk in lonely places alone. choose a brighter and busier road

attract attention when sexually harassed in public don't hesitate to shout, ask for help or code to get other people's attention

Be aware of the surrounding environment whether on nets, in public transportation, or also in public places.

make sure you have set an emergency phone number if someone is suspicious, you can immediately contact your parents, friends, or the police quickly

always have personal protection available, not firearms or sharp objects, but objects that can be used for self-defense such as umbrellas, chili spray, or electric shocks

Don't be afraid to record or take photos of suspicious people. this way can be as evidence if the authorities ask for evidence.

Consent Beacon

Consent Beacon was developed by university students who are deeply concerned about harassment, particularly sexual harassment. We are dedicated to helping victims by offering a platform for victims in need of any type of assistance they necessitate, ranging from reliable report form with professional responder to huge data of help centers. We also want to provide an environment for anyone who wants to learn more about sexual harassment by providing an easy to understand education page and blogs that provides a curated and trusted studies from experts
